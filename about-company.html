О компании

YOUR SUCCESS IS OUR PRIORITY

Antynea Form products are part of a significant number of objects that surround us in everyday life, meaning that our quality guarantor determines the comfort of life for many people.

Being one of the leading polyurethane foam manufacturers in the CIS, Antynea Form is responsible for its products and provides high-quality business solutions.

Quality & Innovation

The specialized knowledge accumulated over decades, advanced automated production, own lab, and multi-level control ensure consistently high standards of the products.

A qualified engineering staff monitors the entire production cycle from recipe creation to quality control of finished goods.

The company uses exclusively high-quality raw materials from world-class time-tested manufacturers.

Building partnership

The sales team will promptly provide an individual approach in any convenient way at any convenient time.

Supplier reliability is a crucial element of any company's success and sustainability.

Antynea Form is guaranteed to save you time and resources with constant support throughout the entire interaction cycle.

The R&D department is constructing the future of foam production, meeting all sorts of customer and market requirements.

Smooth logistics & Comprehensive geographical coverage

Antynea Form also delivers in small batches and offers free storage in the warehouses.

Numerous offices in Warsaw, Moscow, and Minsk along with the truck fleet capable of transporting over 500 m³ of products per week, allow us to solve the main logistic challenges of the client as quickly and reliably as possible.

Health & Environmental protection

Let us take care of the future together: paper packing is available!

Antynea Form shares the European principles of environmental friendliness of production and products. The enterprise strictly adheres to the requirements for labor safety and protection and uses only modern treatment facilities.

Antynea Form has organized separate collection and processing of production waste.
